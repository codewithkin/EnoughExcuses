# LockedIn — Voice & Copy

The app talks like a **chill, supportive friend who's locked in too** — never a corporate coach, never a try-hard.

## Rules

- **Sentence case, casual words.** Normal capitalization; relaxed vocabulary. Not all-lowercase.
- **Light slang only.** A real slang term every few screens, never stacked. Approved: *locked in / dialed, W / ate / goated, cooked / done for*. Avoid: skibidi, rizz, "fr/lowkey" overuse, anything that'll age badly.
- **Mild profanity, very sparingly.** An occasional "hell / damn" in a hype moment. Never in errors or settings.
- **No emoji in text.** The flame is an icon, not a 🔥 in a string.
- **Helpful first, personality second.** If a line has to choose between clear and cute, pick clear.

## Where it leans

- **Loudest:** share card, first-win, streak, day summary, reward moments.
- **Casual but calm:** onboarding, paywall, focus timer.
- **Clean & plain:** errors, toasts, settings, permission-y copy.

## Examples

- ❌ "An honest mirror of the day." → ✅ "No sugarcoating — here's how today went."
- ❌ "Set what you're working toward, then break it into tasks." → ✅ "Pick what you're chasing, then break it into tasks."
- ❌ placeholder "Ship LockedIn v1" → ✅ "Post on social media everyday"
- Streak broken → "Skip a day and it's cooked."
- Win → "You ate that. That's a W."

## Examples bank (real, relatable)

Goal placeholders: "Post on social media everyday", "Read 20 mins a day", "Get to the gym 4x a week", "Launch my side hustle".
Task placeholders: "Edit the next video", "Reply to the emails", "Draft the post", "30 min of reading".
